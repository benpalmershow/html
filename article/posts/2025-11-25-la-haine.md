### <i data-lucide='film' class='post-icon'></i> **La Haine**

<a href="media.html#la-haine"><img src="https://media.themoviedb.org/t/p/w500/8rgPyWjYZhsphSSxbXguMnhN7H0.jpg" alt="La Haine" style="width: 60px; height: auto; float: left; margin-right: 10px;"></a>

Mathieu Kassovitz's 1995 masterpiece follows three young friends—led by <a href="https://www.imdb.com/name/nm0001993/?ref_=ext_shr_lnk" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Vincent Cassel</a>—navigating the aftermath of a brutal police incident in a Parisian suburb. Shot in stark black-and-white, the film captures 24 hours of systemic tension, urban rage, and the quiet desperation of those living on society's margins.
