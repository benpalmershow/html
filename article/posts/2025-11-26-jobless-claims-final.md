### **Jobless Claims**

Initial jobless claims (seasonally adjusted, monthly average) fell to 217,600 in November, down from 226,750 in October, marking a meaningful improvement in labor market health. The decline suggests employers remain cautious with layoffs despite economic headwinds.

Claims below 300,000 typically indicate a resilient job market. The recent decline reinforces signals that employers are holding onto workers even as consumer sentiment remains fragile, keeping the labor market as one of the economy's strongest anchors.
