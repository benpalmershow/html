### **Vermiglio**

<a href="media.html#vermiglio"><img src="https://media.themoviedb.org/t/p/w500/zNEFWWS495ZWe8Jj5jTPS4zu9IF.jpg" alt="Vermiglio" style="width: 60px; height: auto; float: left; margin-right: 10px;"></a>

Set in a remote mountain village in the Italian Alps during the waning days of WWII, director Maura Delpero crafts a powerful portrait of a family fractured by war and desire. When a Sicilian deserter arrives, his forbidden romance with the eldest daughter unleashes a cascade of moral questions that reverberate through the tightly-knit village.
