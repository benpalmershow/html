### **Shipping Volumes Down**

Container shipping volumes at the Port of LA/Long Beach show moderate activity levels, with October volumes at 848,424 TEUs compared to September's 883,053 TEUs. The data reflects typical seasonal patterns in global trade flows, with the port complex handling about 40% of U.S. imports. These TEU volumes serve as a key indicator of trade conditions, consumer demand patterns, and overall supply chain health.
