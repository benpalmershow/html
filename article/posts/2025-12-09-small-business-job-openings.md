### <i data-lucide='briefcase' class='post-icon'></i> **Small Business Optimism**

Small business confidence climbed in November, with the NFIB index signaling continued optimism about hiring plans and capital spending despite economic headwinds.

- **Small Business Optimism Index**: November 99.0 (+0.8% MoM)

The modest monthly gain reflects improved sentiment heading into year-end, with the 10-component index holding near expansion territory (100+). The index measures small business expectations on employment, inventory, and capital spending, providing an early read on labor market dynamics and business investment trends across the economy.
