###  **War, Reparations, and Dying**

## <i data-lucide='ship' class='post-icon'></i> Build Ships

Congress's annual defense authorization includes $30.96 billion ($20.84 billion requested). The DDG–51 destroyer program receives $6.34 billion in hull construction plus $5.40 billion additional for "workforce and shipyard enhancements".

[Read: S.2296, §4101, FY2026 Defense Authorization](news.html?article=ndaa-fy2026-s2296)

## <i data-lucide='landmark' class='post-icon'></i> **Reparations Fund**

San Francisco formally established a Reparations Fund—turning a political idea into a budget structure with defined legal plumbing. The ordinance creates the fund to receive appropriated or donated dollars, but it does not set a specific appropriation amount. No spending authority follows without a separate vote.

[Read: San Francisco Administrative Code Amendment](news.html?article=administrative-code-reparations-fund)
