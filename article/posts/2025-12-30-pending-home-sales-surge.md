### <i data-lucide='home' class='post-icon'></i> **Pending Home Sales Surge to 79.2**

Pending home sales index jumped to 79.2 in November, the strongest reading since March and a 3.3% gain from October's 76.7. This leading indicator suggests existing home sales will continue gaining momentum through year-end.

- **November**: 79.2 (+3.3% MoM)
- **October**: 76.7

The index measures signed contracts on existing homes, leading actual sales by 1-2 months. The November surge indicates more buyers moved to closing on properties despite higher interest rates, signaling improving buyer sentiment and reduced inventory constraints heading into 2026.
