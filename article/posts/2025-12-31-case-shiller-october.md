### <i data-lucide='home' class='post-icon'></i>**Case-Shiller Home Price Index Declines in October**

Case-Shiller National Home Price Index fell to 328.442, down **0.2% MoM** from 328.978. The fourth consecutive monthly decline reflects ongoing seasonal softening in the housing market as higher mortgage rates continue to suppress buyer demand.

**October**: 328.442 (-0.2% MoM) | **September**: 328.978

### **Soda SNAP**

New SNAP retailer compliance policies take effect January 1, 2026, implementing stricter restrictions on soda purchases. The updated USDA guidelines prohibit carbonated soft drinks with added sugars, energy drinks with added sweeteners, and sweetened bottled teas and fruit drinks.
