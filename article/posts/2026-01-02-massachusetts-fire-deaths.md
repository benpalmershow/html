### <i data-lucide='alert-circle' class='post-icon'></i> **Massachusetts Fire Deaths Drop 12% in 2025**

Massachusetts recorded 44 fire deaths in 2025, a decline from 50 in 2024. More than 90% of fatalities occurred at home, with seniors aged 65+ accounting for 55% of victims despite representing just 17% of the population.

**Key Statistics:**
- **2024 Deaths:** 50 fatalities
- **2025 Deaths:** 44 fatalities (12% decline)
- **Children Lost:** 1 (down from 3 in 2024)
- **Seniors (65+):** 24 deaths (55% of fatalities)

The Gabriel House Assisted Living fire in Fall River claimed 10 lives in a single incident - the deadliest Massachusetts fire in decades. Smoking materials remained the leading cause in 21 fire deaths, particularly dangerous when combined with medical oxygen use. Only 16 of 30 residential fires with determinable smoke alarm status had working alarms.
