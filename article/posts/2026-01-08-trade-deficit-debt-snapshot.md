### <i data-lucide='package' class='post-icon'></i> **Trade Deficit Collapses to $29.4B**

The October trade deficit plummeted to $29.4 billion from $48.1 billion in September (-39.0% MoM), marking the tightest trade balance in recent months. Imports dropped to $331.4 billion while exports surged to $302.0 billion, signaling a sharp shift in trade dynamics.
