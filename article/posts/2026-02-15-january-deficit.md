### <i data-lucide='landmark' class='post-icon'></i> **January Deficit Down to $94.6B**

January's monthly budget deficit came in at $94.6 billion, the smallest monthly deficit since last spring as tax revenue surged to $560 billion and interest payments fell to $76 billion.

**January 2026:** -$94.6B (tax revenue $560B, interest on debt $76B)

The federal government collected $560 billion in tax revenue in January, up sharply from December's $484B (+15.6% MoM), while interest payments on the national debt dropped to $76 billion from $92 billion (-17.4% MoM).
