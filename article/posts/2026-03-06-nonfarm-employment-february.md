### <i data-lucide='users' class='post-icon'></i> **Jobs: -92k**

Total nonfarm employment declined to 158.466 million in February, down 92,000 from January's revised 158.558 million.

**December (revised):** 158.432 million  
**January (revised):** 158.558 million  
**February:** 158.466 million  
**Monthly Change:** -92,000 (-0.1% MoM)

The February decline follows significant downward revisions to previous months. January's job growth was revised down by 69,000 from the initially reported 158.627 million, while December was revised down by 65,000 from the originally reported 158.497 million. The cumulative revisions of 134,000 across two months indicate the labor market was weaker than initially reported, with current employment levels 42,000 below December's revised figure.
