### <i data-lucide='bridge' class='post-icon'></i> **B1 Bridge Struck**

<a href='https://x.com/monitorx99800/status/2040389965629002214' target='_blank' rel='noopener noreferrer'>Source</a> | <a href='https://x.com/jansant/status/2040085820477145265' target='_blank' rel='noopener noreferrer'>Additional</a>

U.S. forces struck twice and severely damaged the B1 road bridge linking Tehran with Karaj in Alborz Province. The bridge, a critical infrastructure link northwest of Tehran, was hit on April 3. Satellite imagery shows significant structural damage to the span, effectively severing one of the main transportation corridors connecting the capital with the Alborz Province region. This follows a series of U.S. strikes against Iranian infrastructure in recent weeks.
