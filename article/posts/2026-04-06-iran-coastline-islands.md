### <i data-lucide='anchor' class='post-icon'></i> **Iran's Strategic Coastline**

Al Jazeera published an interactive visual guide to Iran's coastline and strategic islands. The piece highlights the strategic importance of Iran's maritime position in the Persian Gulf and Gulf of Oman.

Key points of interest:
- The Strait of Hormuz, through which ~20% of global oil passes
- Iran's various islands in the Persian Gulf
- The strategic significance of coastal geography in regional geopolitics
