### <i data-lucide='scale' class='post-icon'></i> **NYC's Preliminary Racial Equity Plan**

New York City has released its Preliminary Racial Equity Plan, charting community equity priorities and outcome indicators across multiple city agencies. 

While the stated goal is addressing historical inequities in areas like education, housing, and criminal justice, the extensive bureaucratic apparatus required to monitor and enforce these outcomes feels inherently inefficient. The city's persistent focus on categorizing residents and prioritizing specific demographic groups raises questions about the long-term economic and social implications for a metropolis that fundamentally requires meritocratic engines of growth.
