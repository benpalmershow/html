### <i data-lucide='landmark' class='post-icon'></i> **Trump Accounts Update**

The Treasury has designated BNY Mellon as financial agent to support the new Trump Accounts program. BNY has partnered with Robinhood, which will serve as brokerage and initial trustee.

The app is being developed as a custom, white-label product exclusively for Treasury, with the National Design Studio (partnered with Robinhood) creating the UI/UX. Treasury retains control over the app and operations.

This is a significant step in implementing what appears to be a universal savings program for children. The program represents a notable public-private partnership in the financial services space.
