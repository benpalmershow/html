### <i data-lucide='vote' class='post-icon'></i> **1996 to 2026: Only citizens are eligible to vote**

In 1996, Congress passed the Illegal Immigration Reform and Immigrant Responsibility Act with a 97-3 Senate vote. President Bill Clinton signed it into law.

The bill strengthened border enforcement, mandated employer verification, and explicitly prohibited non-citizens from voting in federal elections. <a href="https://www.congress.gov/bill/119th-congress/house-joint-resolution/152" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Current House joint resolution</a> seeks to reaffirm this same principle.
