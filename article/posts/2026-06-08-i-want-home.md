## I Want Home

Can't get home  
The true dystopia

My Waymo only made right turns  
(I couldn't tell it otherwise)

My airplane backseat screen is on an infinite ad loop.  
None of the actors act like humans.

Are they aliens?  
They behave so  

I'm spiraling

I immediately realize the only way home requires an app

I notice there are no humans everywhere I look  
We're already extinct!

My phone battery is dying

Now my heart is racing - not because of my phone  
and its imminent lack of utility;  
I control that and I've mitigated it with electing to go yellow - battery save mode.  
I've got it under control.

But  it's not my device I can't control.

It's the hermetic airport ecosystem with closed options.  
It's the plane with a novice pilot  
The descent is too steep!

Is there a human at the yoke?

I measure my exhale  

6

Inhale, 6

Is AI flying the plane?

Where are the humans?

What happened to his intuition?  
Where's his grace?

In the airport there were no customer service agents

I can't catch an earlier flight without the app  
But the app was glitching

My WIFI?

I can't click the SUBMIT button.  
Javascript? Cookies? Reset? Restart?

It won't accept my identification.  
The computer is zero trust.

Pacing the airport, no one would make eye contact

I just needed a human.  
No one can help me.

I don't want small talk  
I don't want niceties

I give up.

Now I want mean

Now I want to tussle, to feel - anything.

I want to take back my money, I want violence  
I'm angry  
I'm emotional

Everyone seems fine and unemotional.  
I'm not fine.  
I'm struggling with complacency.

What's wrong with me?

Control only what you can control.  
I can't control anything!

The automatic sliding door opens

But the United club is not United support  
They remind me there's an app for that

I keep walking but can't find a gate agent.

On the way, inflated prices for sustenance

I don't want fast food  
I don't want a stomach ache

I don't want to read a boring magazine.  
I don't want to sit by myself.

There's nowhere to sit at the gate  
So I stand for a little  
Then I walk

Every terminal is terminal

There are quilts on the wall  
That is art

I sip water only when someone is not filling their 5 gallon Nalgene  
quenching their non-thirst for their non-hike

The fountain auto sensor needs my hands.  
It's not actually automatic.

Suddenly I notice there are no children.  
Where did they all go?

Then I see nobody has visible ears

Ensconced in Headphones  
Speckled with white AirPods

No eyes  
Just Screens

Heart rate back up  
I'm panicking

I don't scream because I know better  
I'm conditioned  
I'm soft

The police would arrest me, of course

I'm not crazy  
Am I crazy?

Why is this what we want?

If I notice, I'm not welcome  
Lose credit  
Gain debt

My FICO would drop  
My premium would rise  
I would have a record

I was a nuisance  
I disturbed the peace

No one would befriend me

I'd lose any followers I don't have

My job would be at stake  
I wouldn't make no money

To keep FICO up up  
and DEBT down down

AI doesn't want my human

Capital wants my money, my productivity

Society wants my soul

I want dead

No one is listening anyway  
What's the point?

My phone Battery indicator is yellow; 36 percent

Wheels up  
We're flying  
We're so high

Only me and the one three year old are exhilarated  
We're in chairs at 30,000

I want higher  
I want to go to the moon

I haven't seen the stars for ages  
I want to escape  
I want the fuck out

But I can't

I have a family  
I have a job  
I'm a citizen

So I go back  
To my screen  
To angry memes  
To strangers posting online  
identified by alphanumeric characters after an @

Next to jet power with pretzels and water

The automated welcome message comes on

Ding

Prerecorded in perfect English
Please sit down and fasten your seatbelts

Before, at welcome the human novice took her shot  
She read the script like she was supposed to.  
Like a 5 year old learning to read

Ding 

Each pause  
Welcome to the friendly…  
Smoking is…  
Federal law prohibits tampering…

No!!!

I don't want darling alien  
I want human!!      
I want human!!  
I want home!
