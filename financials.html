Data Sources

The following data sources are used to compile the financial indicators and portfolio information:

Government Agencies

  • Bureau of Labor Statistics (BLS) - Employment, inflation, and price data
  • U.S. Census Bureau - Housing, construction, and demographic data
  • Department of Labor (DOL) - Employment and labor statistics
  • U.S. Treasury - Financial and economic data
  • Federal Reserve - Monetary policy and economic indicators
  • Department of Commerce - Economic and business statistics

Financial Data Providers

  • FRED (Federal Reserve Economic Data) - Economic time series data
  • The Conference Board - Leading economic indicators
  • ADP - Employment and payroll data
  • National Association of Realtors - Housing market data
  • Federal Housing Finance Agency - Housing finance data

Economic Indicators

  • Institute for Supply Management (ISM) - Manufacturing and services PMI
  • University of Michigan - Consumer sentiment surveys
  • Manheim - Used vehicle value index
